The Movie Recommendation System is an intelligent engine designed to suggest personalized movie choices based on user preferences, past viewing history, and ratings. Pneumonia Prediction and Detection System

Pneumonia Prediction and Detection System is an AI-powered medical tool that analyzes chest X-ray images to detect signs of pneumonia. Using deep learning techniques, the model can accurately classify images into categories such as normal or pneumonia. This system aims to provide quick and reliable diagnoses, reducing the burden on healthcare professionals and improving patient care, especially in remote or underserved areas.
